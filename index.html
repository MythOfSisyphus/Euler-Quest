My college computer teacher asked all students to send him two C++ programs daily. I liked the idea but I don't want to write simple programs and I have done enough LeetCode problems in the past and I don't want to do that again then I remembered I always wanted to do Project Euler problems.

I enjoy discussing problems and ways to approach them that's why I am also documenting my whole journey. I know in this journey I will be studying Wikipedia article, browsing StackOverflow and brainstorming with ChatGPT and that will broaden my horizon and level me up. I am ready for this quest!
